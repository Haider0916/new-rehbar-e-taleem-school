New Rehbar e Taleem School

Honouring tradition, inspiring tomorrow. Education from Pre‑Nursery through Matriculation.

At Rehbar e Taleem School, we nurture curiosity, inspire confidence, and promote academic excellence for students from pre-nursery to matriculation. From a child’s very first day on campus, we strive to create a welcoming and stimulating environment where learning feels exciting, purposeful, and meaningful. Our goal is not only to teach lessons from textbooks, but to spark a lifelong love of discovery that empowers students to think independently and grow into capable, responsible individuals. At the core of Rehbar e Taleem School is our team of compassionate, qualified, and dedicated teachers. They serve not only as educators, but also as mentors and role models who guide students academically and personally. By understanding each learner’s strengths, interests, and unique pace of development, our teachers tailor their approach to help every child reach their full potential. Through continuous professional development and a commitment to innovative teaching practices, our faculty ensures that instruction remains dynamic, relevant, and effective. Beyond academics, we place a strong emphasis on character building and values education. We aim to cultivate integrity, empathy, resilience, and a sense of social responsibility in every learner. Through classroom discussions, community initiatives, and leadership opportunities, students learn the importance of teamwork, respect, and ethical decision-making. We believe that strong character forms the foundation for success in higher education and in life.

Admissions

To apply, download the fee structure (PDF) and the admission form. Fill out the form completely with accurate information and attach all required documents (see below).

Applications are accepted during school hours (8 am–3 pm) at the main office. You can also email scanned copies to newrehbaretaleem@gmail.com if an email address is available.

Documents Required

  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school records (if any)
  • Photographs (4 recent passport-size)
  • Proof of residence (utility bill, rent agreement)
  • Parent/guardian identification

Note: keep photocopies – originals will be returned after verification.

Life at Rehbar e Taleem

Life at Rehbar e Taleem School is active, balanced, and student-centered. Alongside regular classroom learning, students take part in assemblies, co-curricular activities, academic competitions, and community initiatives that help them grow in confidence and responsibility. From pre-nursery to matriculation, we encourage participation, teamwork, and healthy interaction so that every child feels involved and valued. Our aim is to create a school environment where students not only learn, but also build friendships, develop skills, and enjoy their daily school experience.

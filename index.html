Honouring tradition, inspiring tomorrow. Education from Pre‑Nursery through Matriculation.

Rehbar e Taleem School offers a nurturing environment where every child—from pre‑nursery to matric—finds a place to learn, grow and belong. Small class sizes mean individual attention, while our committed faculty bring energy and experience to every lesson. Whether you're interested in academics, arts, or athletics, there is a space here for your child to thrive.

Achievements

  • Established in 1995 as the first private school in the area.
  • Over 10 000 students have graduated since opening.
  • Pioneered co‑curricular activities and modern teaching methods in the region.

Admissions

To apply, download the fee structure (PDF) and the admission form. Fill out the form completely with accurate information and attach all required documents (see below).

Applications are accepted during school hours (8 am–3 pm) at the main office. You can also email scanned copies to admissions@rehbar.example.com if an email address is available.

Documents Required

  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school records (if any)
  • Photographs (4 recent passport-size)
  • Proof of residence (utility bill, rent agreement)
  • Parent/guardian identification

Note: keep photocopies – originals will be returned after verification.
